Naples Style Pizza

Try out our delicioous recipe for naples style Pizza!

The recipe we have aquired from a proud grandma living her life in the sicilian hills.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Tomatosauce
  • Mozerella
  • Basil

Steps:

  1. Mix Flour, Salt and Water together in a pot and let it rest for 2h.
  2. Prepare the other ingredientts while the dough is resting.
  3. After the dough rested, spread it out on a flat surface with your palm and roll it till it get's circular
  4. Add all the ingrediensts, staring with the tomatosauce, folowwed by the mozerella and at the end add the basil leaves
  5. Put it into the oven (240Â°C) for 2 minutes and youre done.
